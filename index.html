Welcome to the Crystal Collector Game!

This is a guessing game!

There are four crystals displayed as buttons below.

You will be shown a number to guess at the start of the game.

When you click on a crystal it will add an amount of points to the your total score.

You win the game if your total score matches the random number. If you go above the random number, you lose.

Good Luck!
