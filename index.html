Welcome to the Crystal Collector Game!

This is a guessing game! There are four crystals displayed as buttons below. You will be shown a random number at the start of the game. When you click on a crystal it will add a specific amount of points to the your total score. You win the game if your total score matches the random number. If you go above the random number, you lose. Your total score is displayed and updated after each crystal is clicked. The number of games won and lost will be displayed.
